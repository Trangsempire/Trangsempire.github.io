Yokato Yokabai

I don’t know if you can tell already but I’m a die hard fan of ramen. Like ANY type of ramen. I love them all <3 It’s always been my favourite thing when I learn about a new ramen restaurant opening in Montreal. But from all the dozens of restaurants I tried, nothing beats the ramen from Yokato Yokabai.
This will be a very big statement but I think this is the BEST ramen restaurant so far. I always use this ramen for reference when I review the other places because nothing has ever come close to this perfection. The broth is very special, it’s creamy and they add their own twist to the already knowned pork bone Hakana Tonkotsu. The flavour stays and is engrained in your memory. You will be wanting for more. I always come back to this restaurant whenever I feel like treating myself.

Every Ingredient is Gold

The menu is pretty simple, you get what you need. By filling out your choices, you can choose between pork, chicken and vegan. Usually, I always find fault in the meat. They either failed the pork and the beef is amazing or vice versa. But in here, both pork AND the chicken were delicious. The chicken was very juicy and moist. The pork was fatty, thick and well marinated. The noodles are homemade, straight thin noodles that feels like eating noodle clouds hahah I love it.
But the best part guys? THE EGGS. I AM OBSSESSED WITH THEIR MARINATED EGGS.

There Will Be A Big Line

I mean, what do you expect from the best ramen in town? LOL but usually if you don’t want to wait in line, you should come right when they open at noon for lunch. For supper, it’s tricky because there’s already a line around 6:30PM so be prepared to wait haha.

4185 Drolet, Montreal, QC H2W 2L5
